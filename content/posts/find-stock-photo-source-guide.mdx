# Use Case: Finding Stock Photo Sources

Identifying stock photo sources helps you understand licensing and purchase proper rights. This guide shows you how.

## Why Find Stock Sources?

Finding sources helps you:
- Understand licensing
- Purchase proper licenses
- Verify usage rights
- Find photographers
- Get high-res versions

## Process

1. Reverse search the image
2. Look for stock site results
3. Check watermarked versions
4. Verify licensing terms
5. Purchase if needed

## Common Sites

- Shutterstock
- Getty Images
- Adobe Stock
- iStock
- Unsplash

## Best Practices

- Always verify licensing
- Check usage terms
- Purchase proper licenses
- Attribute when required
