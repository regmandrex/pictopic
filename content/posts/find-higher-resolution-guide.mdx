# Use Case: Finding Higher Resolution Versions

Finding higher resolution images is crucial for printing, presentations, and professional use. This guide shows you how.

## Why Higher Resolution?

Higher resolution is needed for:
- Printing
- Professional presentations
- Website hero images
- Social media graphics
- Design projects

## Finding Process

1. Search with [reverse image search tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links)
2. Look for size indicators
3. Check "View Original" links
4. Visit source websites
5. Download high-res versions

## Best Sources

- Original uploads
- Stock photo sites
- Official websites
- Social media originals
- Press releases

## Tips

- Check dimensions
- Verify not upscaled
- Look for "original" links
- Try multiple providers
