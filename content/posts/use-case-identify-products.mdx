# Use Case: Identifying Products in Images

Identifying products in images helps you find items, compare prices, and discover brands. This guide shows you how to identify products.

## Why Identify Products?

Product identification helps you:
- Find products to purchase
- Compare prices
- Discover brands
- Learn about items
- Find alternatives

## Identifying Products

1. **Upload Product Image**: Use [reverse image search tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links)
2. **Look for Shopping Links**: Check for product results
3. **Identify Brands**: Find brand information
4. **Compare Prices**: Check different retailers
5. **Find Alternatives**: Look for similar products

## Best Practices

- Focus on product features
- Look for logos and branding
- Check shopping results
- Compare multiple sources
- Verify product information
