# How to Find Stock Photo Sources

Identifying stock photo sources helps you understand licensing and usage rights. This guide shows you how to find stock photo origins.

## Why Find Stock Photo Sources?

Finding sources helps you:
- Understand licensing terms
- Purchase proper licenses
- Verify usage rights
- Find higher resolutions
- Contact photographers

## Finding Stock Photo Sources

### Step 1: Reverse Image Search

Upload the image to [reverse image search tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links) and look for:
- Stock photo site results
- Watermarked versions
- Licensing information
- Photographer credits

### Step 2: Check Stock Databases

Search major stock sites:
- Shutterstock
- Getty Images
- Adobe Stock
- iStock
- Unsplash

### Step 3: Verify Licensing

Once found:
- Check license type
- Review usage terms
- Note attribution requirements
- Understand restrictions

## Common Stock Photo Sites

- **Shutterstock**: Large collection, various licenses
- **Getty Images**: Premium stock, strict licensing
- **Adobe Stock**: Creative Cloud integration
- **Unsplash**: Free with attribution
- **Pexels**: Free for commercial use

## Best Practices

- Always verify licensing
- Check usage terms carefully
- Purchase proper licenses
- Attribute when required
- Respect copyright

## Legal Considerations

- Stock photos have licenses
- Free doesn't always mean no restrictions
- Check commercial use rights
- Verify attribution requirements
- Understand modification rights
