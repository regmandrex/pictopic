# Use Case: Finding Higher Resolution Versions

Finding higher resolution images is crucial for printing, presentations, and professional use. This guide shows you how to locate better quality versions.

## Why Find Higher Resolution?

Higher resolution helps with:
- Professional printing
- Large displays
- Website hero images
- Social media graphics
- Design projects

## Finding Higher Resolution

1. **Search Your Image**: Use [reverse image search tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links)
2. **Check Dimensions**: Look for larger pixel dimensions
3. **Find Originals**: Check original source websites
4. **Check Stock Sites**: Look for stock photo sources
5. **Download High-Res**: Get the best quality available

## Best Practices

- Check image dimensions in results
- Look for "View Original" links
- Try stock photo databases
- Check original upload pages
- Verify it's not upscaled
