# Use Case: Finding People Online

Finding where people's photos appear online can be useful for various legitimate purposes. This guide covers responsible use of face search.

## Legitimate Uses

- Finding your own photos online
- Locating missing persons (authorities)
- Verifying identity
- Research purposes
- Social media discovery

## Finding People Online

1. **Use Face Search**: Upload face photo to [reverse image search tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links)
2. **Try Yandex**: Best for face recognition
3. **Check Social Media**: Look for profile matches
4. **Review Results**: Verify matches carefully
5. **Respect Privacy**: Use responsibly

## Best Tools

- [Yandex Images](/providers/yandex) - Excellent for faces
- [Google Images](/providers/google) - General search
- [Reverse Image Search Tool](/tools/reverse-image-search-links) - Multi-provider

## Privacy Considerations

- Respect privacy rights
- Use only for legitimate purposes
- Don't stalk or harass
- Follow platform rules
- Consider consent

## Responsible Use

Always use face search responsibly and ethically. Respect privacy and only use for legitimate purposes.
